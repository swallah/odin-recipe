Jollof

The is an original Ghanian Jollof Rice

Ingridients

  • Long grain Rice
  • Oil
  • vigies/tomatoe sauce
  • Chicken

Steps to cooking it:

  1. wash the rise in clean water
  2. Cut vigies to the prefered size.
  3. fry them in oil and add spieces of choice for flavor and taste
  4. fry seasoned chicken to golden brown
  5. Add fried ripe plantain
